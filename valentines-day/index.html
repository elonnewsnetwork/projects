A note from the editor

With Valentine's Day falling on a Wednesday — the day The Pendulum publishes every week — we wanted to explore the different ways Elon University students experience the holiday. In this edition are stories involving the LGBTQIA community and long distance relationships. There’s also a guide to some of the best date restaurants around Elon and cut out Valentine’s Day cards — just like elementary school. We hope you enjoy it.

-Emmanuel Morgan, Managing Editor
