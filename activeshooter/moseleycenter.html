Run
The student activity side of Moseley Student Center has multiple entrances and exits. There is no reason to hide or fight if a gunman enters this facility. Run in the opposite direction of the perceieved threat. If it is unclear where the gunman is, use context clues to identify the direction of the threat.
Run
When attempting to escape, run from structure to structure to avoid direct hits and richochettes from bullets.
Hide
If the gunman enters the building before you are able to escape and are trapped in Moseley Center Offices, immediately turn off the lights and lock the door. Attempt to barricade the door with movable furniture, but only do so if noise is down.
Hide
Do not use an elevator during an active shooter situation. If you hide in an elevator you limit your escape routes and if you use it, the noise may attract the attention of the gunman.
