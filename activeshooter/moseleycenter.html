RUN
The student activity side of the Moseley Student Center has multiple entrances and exits. There is no reason to hide or fight if a gunman enters this facility. Run in the opposite direction of the perceived threat. If the location of the gunman is unclear, use context clues — running people, sounds of gunfire — to identify the direction of the threat.
RUN
When attempting to escape from an active shooter run from structure to structure in order to avoid direct hits or ricochettes. Pause momentarily between structures and wait for the opportune time — gunman is reloading or is distracted — to dash to the next structure.
HIDE
If you are inside one of the student offices within Moseley Student Center when a gunman enters the facility and are unable to escape, immediately turn off the lights and lock the door. While making the least amount of noise, attempt to barricade the door with furniture. Assume that the gunman will find you, choose a makeshift weapon that is easy to weild. Prepare to defend yourself.
HIDE
If there is an active shooter in Moseley Student Center, do not use the elevator in any situation. Hiding in the elevator would limit your escape routes, making you an easy target for the gunman. The noise from using the elevator may also alert the gunman and lead him to your location.
