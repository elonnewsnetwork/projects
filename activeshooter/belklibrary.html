FIGHT
If you are near the entrance that a gunman uses to enter Carol Grotnes Belk Library, and feel compelled to act, use surrounding furniture and items as makeshift weapons. Ideally, these would be light weight items that would not slow down your mobility — for example, a light chair. This could distract the gunman long enough for others in the library to escape.
RUN
When evading the gunman, run from obstructive structure to obstructive structure to avoid being hit by bullets and ricochettes.
RUN
Only attempt to run to the second or third floor of Carol Grotnes Belk Library if the gunman is pursuing you. The windows in the library are two-paned and do not have an opening mechanism — these are not ideal for escape. There are more than half a dozen exits on the first floor, these would be your ideal routes out of the library.
HIDE
Unless you are trapped or injured, do not hide in the event of an active shooter in Carol Grotnes Belk Library. In facility with multiple exits, your top priority is escaping. While the library has many hiding spots, do not turn yourself into stationary prey.
