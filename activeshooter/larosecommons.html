If a gunman enters an open facility with multiple entrances and exits, like LaRose Student Center, immediately run to the nearest exit in the opposite direction of the perceived threat. If at all possible, try an exit that is not in the gunman’s direct line of site.

If at all possible, avoid exits that are in the gunman's direct line of site. Pinpointing your location or the exit that you used, could make you easy prey for an active shooter.
FIGHT
If you are near the gunman when they enter LaRose Student Center, and are able, surprise them as they remove their hands from the firearm to open the door. Choose a staging point for your attack that is out of the gunman’s view, in this situation right by the entrance. If you have time, choose a light makeshift weapon that you can wield easily. If this is not possible, target the gunman's eyes, throat or groin. Keep the barrel of the gun pointed away from you or fleeing individuals at all costs.
