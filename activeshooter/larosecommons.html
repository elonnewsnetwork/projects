FIGHT
If you are near the gunman when they enter LaRose Student Center, and are physically able, surprise them as they open the door. HIDE
If there is an active shooter in LaRose Student Center, do not use the elevator. Hiding in the elevator would limit possible escape routes which would make you an easy target for the gunman. The noise from the elevator may alert the gunman and lead them to your location.
